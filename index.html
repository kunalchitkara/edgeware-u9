U9 Softball Cricket — Custom Rules
Base Score: 200
Every team starts at 200 runs. All scores display in the 200s. Prevents negative totals from wicket deductions.
Partnerships
Players bat in pairs. Each partnership bats exactly 4 overs. 8 players = 4 pairs = 16 overs per innings.
Total Overs
Total overs = 2 x number of players. 8 players = 16 overs, 9 = 18, 10 = 20. Both teams bat once.
Wickets (−5 runs)
A wicket deducts 5 runs from the team score. The batting pair keeps playing — they switch ends but continue batting.
Wides & No-Balls (+2)
Wides and No-Balls add 2 runs to the batting team’s score. Not re-bowled in normal overs. Extra runs ran are added on top.
Last Over Special
In the final over only, Wides and No-Balls are re-bowled. Up to 3 extra deliveries (B7, B8, B9) available.
Byes & Leg Byes
Byes (Δ) and Leg Byes (v) count as team runs only. They do NOT count toward the individual batsman’s personal score.
Bowler Credit
Bowlers get wicket credit for: B, C, S, L, H. Run Outs (R) do NOT credit the bowler.
