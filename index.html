📖 Match Format & Scoring Rules
🏏 Format
8–10 players per side. Total overs = 2× players (8 = 16 overs, 10 = 20 overs). Both teams bat once.
👥 Partnerships
Players bat in pairs. Each partnership bats 4 overs. 8 players = 4 partnerships = 16 overs.
200 Base Score
Every team starts at 200 runs to avoid negative scores. All final scores display in the 200s.
🔴 Wicket (B/C/S/L/H)
−5 runs from team score. Bowler gets credit. Partnership keeps batting and switches ends.
🔴 Run Out (R)
−5 runs from team score. No bowler credit. Partnership keeps batting and switches ends.
+ Wide
+2 runs to team (not batsman). Compound: +2 = 4 runs total, +4 = 6 runs total. Not re-bowled except last over.
O No-Ball
+2 runs to team (not batsman). Compound: O2 = 4 runs total. Not re-bowled except last over.
∆ Bye / v Leg Bye
Runs to team only, NOT to batsman. ∆ alone = 2 runs. ∆2 = 2 runs total (the number IS the total). v = 2 runs. v2 = 2 runs total.
⭐ Last Over Rule
Final over only: Wides and No-Balls ARE re-bowled (up to 3 extra deliveries: B7–B9). Wide/NB penalty = +1 run in last over only.
