📚 U9 Softball — Match Rules & Scoring Guide
Format
Both teams bat once. Total overs = 2 × number of players (8 players = 16 overs, up to 10 = 20 overs).
🤝 Batting Pairs
Players bat in pairs. Each pair bats for 4 overs. With 8 players, 4 pairs × 4 overs = 16 overs total.
🔴 Starting Score
Every team starts with 200 runs to avoid negative totals. All runs are added to or subtracted from this base.
+ Wide = +2 runs
A wide adds 2 runs to the batting team. Ball is NOT re-bowled (except in the last over). Runs off a wide (+2 = 4 total) are extras only — not credited to the batsman.
O No Ball = +2 runs
A no ball adds 2 runs to the batting team. Ball is NOT re-bowled (except in the last over). Extra runs off a no ball (O2 = 4 total) are extras only.
Δ Bye / v Leg Bye
Byes and leg byes: the number entered IS the total runs (Δ2 = 2 runs total, not 3). These are team extras only — not credited to the batsman.
🔴 Wicket = −5 runs
Any wicket (B/C/S/L/H) deducts 5 runs from the batting team. The pair keeps playing and switches ends. Bowler gets credit for B, C, S, L, H — but NOT run outs.
R Run Out = −5 runs
A run out also deducts 5 runs. The fielder who effected it gets fielding credit. The bowler does NOT get a wicket.
📅 Last Over Special
In the final over only: wides and no balls are +1 run (not +2) and ARE re-bowled. Up to 3 extra balls (B7, B8, B9) available.
📈 Scoring Symbols
B=Bowled   C=Caught   S=Stumped   L=LBW   H=Hit Wicket   R=Run Out   +=Wide   O=No Ball   Δ=Bye   v=Leg Bye   0=Dot ball
📊 Compound Extras
+2 = wide + 2 runs ran (4 total)  |  O2 = no ball + 2 runs (4 total)  |  Δ2 = 2 bye runs total  |  v2 = 2 leg bye runs total
